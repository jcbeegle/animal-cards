Unicorn

A group of unicorns is called a blessing.

  • Scientific Name: Equus monoclonius
  • Average Height: 5-7 feet
  • Average Lifespan: Indeterminate
  • Existence: Fictional...or is it?

The unicorn is a legendary creature that has been described since antiquity as a beast often depicted as a white horse-like creature with a large, pointed, spiraling horn projecting from its forehead. Its horn was said to have the power to render poisoned water potable and to heal sickness.
